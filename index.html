The Salzburg International Christian Church is an ecumenical, international congregation of Christian believers. Our church is part of the Evangelical (Protestant) Church of Austria. With other congregations of this denomination we have roots in the Augsburg (Lutheran) and Helvetic (Reformed/Presbyterian) traditions.

We meet every Sunday at 11 AM Our church services are held in the Chapel of the Diakonie-Zentrum, Guggenbichlerstrasse 20, Salzburg-Aigen.

Directions:

By Local Bus
From the main train station in Salzburg (Hauptbahnhof) take Bus No. 3, 5 or 6 to the Mozartsteg/Rudolfskai stop. Transfer to Bus No.7, to the Überfuhrstrasse/Diakoniezentrum. (Time, approx. 18 min.)

By Regional Bus
Regional bus Nr. 160 from the train station (direction Hallein Kornsteinplatz), to Überfuhrstrasse/Diakoniezentrum (ca. 16 min.)

By Local Rail
Local train (S-Bahn) line S3 from Hauptbahnhof to the Salzburg-Aigen Bahnhof (train station), changing to the Nr. 7 bus to the Überfuhrstrasse/Diakoniezentrum (19 min.)

By Bike
Cycle along the Salzach river going south not the side of old town the other side! until you reach a signpost for the Diakoniezentrum. Follow the path to the Diakoniezentrum. The bike ride takes about 10-15mins.
